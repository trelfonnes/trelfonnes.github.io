Understanding my Development Journey

Being a self-taught, solo developer is an arduous but fulfilling process. Understanding where to direct your focus and how to further your knowledge and skillset is like being dropped into a maze with hundreds of directions that interweave and overlap. Yet, the ability to make creation come to life while understanding the systems that support them is like no other experience. Attaching oneself to quality developers online while grasping for boot camps and conferences is key to finding guidance and direction – not to mention countless attempts of trial and error.
